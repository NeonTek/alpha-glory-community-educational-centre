Our Story

Our Founder

A Vision for a Brighter Future

Alpha Glory was born from the passion and dedication of Mrs. Elizabeth Mbati, a professional teacher with over three decades of experience. Witnessing countless children in her community of Kabiria unable to attend school due to poverty, she was moved to action. She believed that education was the key to unlocking their innate power and overcoming life's challenges.

"We decided the children needed more than just food. They needed an education through which life skills would be imparted to them, helping them tap their power to overcome poverty, hunger, and disease." - Mrs. Elizabeth Mbati
Our History

Growth and Milestones

2009: The Beginning

Alpha Glory starts as a self-help group, with Mrs. Mbati holding literacy classes for a handful of children in a rented wooden premise.

End of 2009: Rapid Growth

Word spreads about the quality of care and education. In its first year, the center grows from just one student to over 75.

Today: A Pillar of the Community

Alpha Glory now serves over 300 learners, providing holistic education, meals, and a safe haven for children from diverse backgrounds.
