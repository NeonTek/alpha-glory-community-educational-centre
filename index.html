Who We Are

From a Simple Idea to a Thriving Community

Alpha Glory began in 2009 from the determined vision of our Director, Mrs. Elizabeth Mbati. A teacher with over 30 years of experience, she saw too many children in the Kabiria community kept from school by poverty. She knew they needed more than just a mealâ€”they needed an education to break the cycle.

What started with a few children in a rented wooden room has blossomed into a thriving educational center for over 300 students. We are a testament to the power of community and the belief that every child deserves the chance to flourish.
