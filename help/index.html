Your Trust Matters

Our Commitment to Transparency

We are deeply committed to ensuring your generosity is used effectively. We provide fully costed budgets for our projects and supply frequent, detailed reports to keep you fully informed on the progress and impact of your contribution.

Have Questions?

We Have Answers

How is my financial donation used?

Your donation covers annual school fees, toiletries, meals, recreational activities, and administrative costs that keep the center running smoothly and effectively.

What are the requirements to volunteer?

We welcome volunteers with various skills. The primary requirement is a passion for helping children and a commitment to our values. Please contact us to discuss how your specific skills can be best utilized.

Can I visit the school?

Yes! We love having supporters visit. Please contact us in advance to schedule a visit so we can ensure it is not disruptive to the children's learning schedule and that someone is available to host you.
